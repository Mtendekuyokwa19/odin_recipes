NOODLES

Ingredients and Materials

  • water
  • Noodle pasta
  • spices
  • bowl

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with 3 quarts of water.Bring the water to boiling if desired Add a tea spoon of salt
  2. Add noodle pasta after some time so that the water does not stop boiling
  3. Reduce the heat slightly
  4. stir occasionally,for two minutes until pasta is al dente
  5. Drain the water using a colander and place it back on the source of heat
  6. Add spices and stir
  7. Add a spoonful of oil
