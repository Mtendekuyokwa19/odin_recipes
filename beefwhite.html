GROUND BEEF:Thai basil beef

Ingredients and materials

  • Neutral oil
  • ground pepper
  • onion
  • soy sauce
  1. Whisk a quick sauce:chili paste,soy sauce,fish sauce,brown sugar and lime juice.P.S this sauce is good on everything.Marinate chicken in it,drizzle it on fish
  2. Crank the heat! When your oil is nice and hot,begin to stir-fry the beef.After a few minutes add the vegitables
  3. Pour the sauce over the stir-fried beef and veggies
  4. serve with white or brown rice
